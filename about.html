About Me

Welcome! I'm an AI Automation Engineer who started in DevOps and discovered my passion for building intelligent, self-optimizing systems.

My journey began with cloud infrastructure and CI/CD pipelines, where I spent 2+ years mastering AWS, Azure, Docker, Kubernetes, and Infrastructure as Code. I loved the efficiency gains, but I kept asking: "What if our systems could learn and adapt on their own?"

That question led me down the path of AI and machine learning. I realized that combining my DevOps foundation with AI capabilities could create something powerful—intelligent automation that doesn't just follow scripts, but actually understands context, predicts issues, and optimizes itself.
